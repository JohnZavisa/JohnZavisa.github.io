Me

I'm John Zavisa, a computer nerd and musician in Nashville, Tennessee.

I currently work as a Software Engineer Intern for AUTOFLYTE, a marketing intelligence company located in Brentwood, TN. I'm graduating from Middle Tennessee State University in Fall of 2018 with a B.S in Computer Science and a minor in Philosophy. I plan on going to graduate school.

My main interests include algorithm design, data analysis, deep learning, and my cats. I spend most of my time coding for work, or studying computer stuff. I play guitar everyday, and have been for many years. I attended Vanderbilt University for Classical Guitar and used to give guitar lessons.
